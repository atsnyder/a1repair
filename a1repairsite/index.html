Comprehensive auto repair

A-1 Repair is an all in one automotive repair shop. We do everything from oil changes to engine and transmission replacement. We even work on diesel trucks and cars.

Semis and Heavy Duty Trucks

A-1 Repair also does more light duty work on semis and heavy duty trucks. We do both scheduled service and same day interstate service depending on the level of repairs required.

24 Hour Towing

Our trucks are on call 24/7 to tow anything you need day or night. We are available for both emergency and non-emergency tows and can tow tractors and equipment. Our trucks can even wench vehicles out of ditches or cornfields.

Tractors and Farm Equipment

We really do work on everything. This includes tractors, and not just new tractors but antique ones as well. We also service farm equipment depending on the level of repairs needed.
